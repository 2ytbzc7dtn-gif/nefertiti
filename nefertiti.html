"MY LOVE IS UNIQUE AND NO ONE CAN COMPETE WITH IT. JUST BY THE WAY, SHE’S ALREADY STOLEN MY HEART." - NEFERTITI

Queen Nefertiti is renowned for her exemplary leadership and deep compassion for her people. Alongside her husband Akhenaton, she worshipped the Sun Disk God, Aton, believed to bestow wisdom and scripture upon them.

Nefertiti's sexuality was emphasized through her exaggerated feminine physique and the elegance of her linen attire.

The marking on Nefertiti's stomach symbolized her profound realization that she would never fulfill the prophecy of bearing a son.

Nefertiti endured tormenting psychic assaults, never knowing when her visions would materialize.

The void in her forehead represents her tragic end.
